Core benefits

Increase Efficiency

RAVN Extract is far more efficient at reading, extracting and summarising information than traditional manual efforts, allowing you to get to the information you are after much quicker. By automating this process, staff that used to manually perform these arduous tasks can now focus on other things.

Mitigates Risk

By automating the review process, companies and firms can reduce risks such as typos and other human errors. Different people interpret data in inconsistent ways, but with the implementation of RAVN Extract, firms can be assured that all the data extracted is consistent.

Increase Margins

Margins are increased as more work can be done in less time. There is no need to hire additional temp staff to meet tight deadlines allowing both labor costs and cost of office space to be reduced. Also, RAVN Extract does not require a promotion, pay rise, overtime or sick days.

What is the Robot?

The RAVN Robot uses Artificial Intelligence technology to automatically read, interpret and extract key information from unstructured data into a desired business output, like a human would do but much faster and eradicating human error.

Relevant information

All you need to do is ask the RAVN Robot what key pieces of information you are looking for (for example addresses, names etc.) and it will automatically read and interpret stacks of documents then extract and summarise the key information for you for in a matter of seconds.

Light speed + Large volume

Organisations are facing a rise in unstructured data and according to CSC the volume of corporate held data will be 35,000,000,000,000 gigabytes by 2020. This unstoppable growth is causing several problems for organisations:

  •   Finding the relevant information
  •   Lack of time and resource to interpret mountains of documents
  •   Lost opportunities due to lack of relevant information
Applied Cogintive Computing

The RAVN Robot is built on top of the RAVN Applied Cognitive Engine (ACE). RAVN ACE brings together different technologies from the fields on Information Processing and AI (Artificial Intelligence) in a coherent, enterprise-ready solution stack. Beyond the foundations of a powerful document search engine, the Applied Cognitive Engine focuses on extracting and distilling the information within documents.
